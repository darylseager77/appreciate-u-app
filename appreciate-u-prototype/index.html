❌ The Problem

Most recognition programs fail because they're too complex. Busy managers face high cognitive load—long surveys, complex dashboards, generic action plans that take hours to implement.

The result? Programs get abandoned. Your best people leave. Teams disengage. You're spending money on tools no one has time to use.

✅ The Solution

Appreciate U helps leaders understand each team member's unique preferences and gives them personalized suggestions.

When people feel valued in the way that matters to them, they stay longer, perform better, and actually want to show up to work.

OTHER PLATFORMS:

  • ❌ Quarterly long surveys → Survey fatigue
  • ❌ Complex dashboards → Hours to learn
  • ❌ Generic action plans → Nothing fits
  • ❌ Constant admin work → Process over people
  • ❌ Top-down approach → People resist

APPRECIATE U:

  • ✅ 25-question quiz → 5 minutes, done once
  • ✅ 30-second check-ins → Quick pulse
  • ✅ Personalized suggestions → Specific to each person
  • ✅ Zero admin work → Automated insights
  • ✅ Team member-driven → People choose to share
